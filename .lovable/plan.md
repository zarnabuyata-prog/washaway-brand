
# Big Wave Truck Wash — Website Plan

## Brand Identity
- **Logo**: The uploaded Big Wave Truck Wash logo will be used in the navbar and footer
- **Color scheme**: Ocean blues and deep greens from the logo, with bold dark backgrounds for hero sections
- **Style**: Bold & modern — large typography, full-width hero images, strong CTAs, inspired by The Giant Truck Wash reference site

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- **Announcement bar** at top (e.g. operating hours)
- **Full-screen hero** with bold headline (e.g. "RIDE THE WAVE OF CLEAN"), dark overlay on a truck wash background image (AI-generated), and a CTA button
- **Services overview section** — cards highlighting Handwash & Automatic Wash with icons and short descriptions
- **Why Choose Us** section with key differentiators (quality, affordability, convenience)
- **Testimonials/trust section**
- **Footer** with contact info, social links, quick navigation

### 2. About Page
- Company story, mission, and values
- Team or facility showcase section with AI-generated imagery
- Stats/counters (vehicles washed, years in business, etc.)

### 3. Hand Wash Page
- Hero banner with AI-generated hand wash imagery
- Detailed description of hand wash services
- List of what's included (brush wash, trailer washouts, detailing, etc.)
- Pricing cards showing different hand wash packages with prices

### 4. Automatic Wash Page
- Hero banner with automatic wash bay imagery
- Description of touchless/automatic wash process
- Pricing tiers in a clean card layout
- "Open 24/7" callout

### 5. Offering Page
- Combined view of all services with descriptions AND pricing tables
- Organized by category (Hand Wash, Automatic Wash, Add-ons)
- Clear pricing cards with feature lists and highlighted "most popular" option

### 6. Vacancies Page
- "Join Our Team" hero section
- Job listings with title, description, requirements, and location
- Each listing has an "Apply Now" button (links to email or external form)
- Company culture/benefits section

### 7. Contact Us Page
- Static contact information: phone, email, address
- Embedded Google Maps placeholder showing location
- Operating hours displayed clearly
- Social media links

## Global Elements
- **Sticky navigation** with logo, page links, and a bold CTA button ("Get a Wash" or "Call Us")
- **Responsive design** — mobile hamburger menu, stacked layouts on smaller screens
- **Smooth scroll animations** for sections fading in
- **Consistent footer** across all pages with navigation, contact info, and social icons

## Technical Notes
- No backend needed — fully static/frontend site
- AI-generated placeholder images for truck wash scenes
- All pages connected via React Router
